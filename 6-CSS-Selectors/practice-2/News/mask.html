Tesla's Elon Musk expects 'robotaxis' to start in US next year


TElon Musk, the chief executive of electric car company Tesla, has said he expects the firm to have self-driving "robotaxis" on the road by 2020. The entrepreneur made the pledge as he announced an improved microchip for its driverless vehicles.
